Our Story

From Adversity to Advocacy. From One Voice to a Movement.

The Rayan Foundation was born from lived experience—a story of resilience, courage, and the unwavering belief that inclusion is not a privilege, but a right.

Our Beginnings

Our journey began in India, in a region where the realities for people with disabilities are harsh and often invisible—just like in many other parts of the world. Inclusive education remains a dream, accessibility a mirage, and support systems are often nonexistent.

These painful experiences weren't just statistics; they were names, stories, and dreams that were never given the chance to grow. But this story didn't end there.

The Turning Point

In search of belonging and opportunity, Rayan immigrated to Nova Scotia, Canada, carrying hope and determination. What began as a personal path of healing quickly transformed into a mission for others.

Our Mission Today

Nova Scotia opened its heart—through its public libraries, community centers, inclusive schools, and support networks. It was here that inclusion stopped being an abstract concept—and became a lived reality. From that lived experience, the Rayan Foundation was born.

What We Do
  • • Supporting newcomer families, especially those navigating disability within unfamiliar systems
  • • Creating inclusive spaces through education, recreation, advocacy, and storytelling
  • • Amplifying underrepresented voices—especially children and youth with disabilities
  • • Driving global awareness beyond borders by sharing knowledge and resources
